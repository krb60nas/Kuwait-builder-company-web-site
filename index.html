About Kuwait Road Builders

Kuwait Road Builders (KRB) is a distinguished and leading construction and infrastructure company firmly established in Kuwait. With a core specialization in road works, our extensive portfolio also encompasses a broad spectrum of vital civil and infrastructure services.

Our Vision

To be a leading and highly recognized provider of premier infrastructure and construction services across the region.

Our Mission

Kuwait Road Builders (KRB) is dedicated to delivering superior infrastructure and construction solutions to clients who value quality and excellence.

Our Services

Roads & Infrastructure Works

As our core specialization, KRB excels in the construction, and maintenance of roads and vital infrastructure. Our capabilities include highway construction, urban road networks, bridge and overpass construction, drainage systems, utility networks, and road maintenance & rehabilitation.

Civil Construction

Our civil construction division undertakes a wide range of projects, demonstrating our versatility and technical prowess. This includes foundational works, structural developments, landscaping & urban development, and specialized concrete works.

Asphalt Plant Operations

KRB operates a state-of-the-art asphalt plant, ensuring a reliable and consistent supply of premium asphalt mixes. Our in-house production capabilities allow us to maintain strict quality control and meet project deadlines efficiently.

Other Specialized Services

Beyond our core offerings, KRB also provides Construction management and quality control & assurance, implementing rigorous protocols to guarantee the highest standards in all our endeavors.
